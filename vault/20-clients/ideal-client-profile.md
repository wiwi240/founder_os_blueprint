# Ideal Client Profile

## Best-Fit Clients

- artisans, consultants and local service businesses
- teams with low digital maturity
- clients needing a landing page, brochure site or light SEO help
- buyers who value speed and clarity more than custom complexity

## Bad-Fit Clients

- complex marketplaces or large custom platforms
- buyers asking for fixed prices with unclear scope
- clients with missing content and no availability

## Coach Signals

- learn how to qualify fit fast
- learn how to frame an MVP in business language
- learn the few questions that expose scope risk early
