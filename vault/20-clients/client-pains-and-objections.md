# Client Pains And Objections

## Recurring Pains

- "We need a site quickly"
- "We do not know what content to prepare"
- "We want more local visibility"
- "We fear paying for something too big"

## Recurring Objections

- price uncertainty
- timeline uncertainty
- doubt about ROI
- confusion between a simple site and a custom product

## Coach Signals

- learn objection handling for price and scope
- learn to explain deliverables in plain language
- learn to tie each feature to a concrete business outcome
