# Decisions Log

## Active Decisions

- choose a local markdown vault first before any SaaS knowledge base
- keep the first Coach agent local and deterministic
- focus the first offer on landing pages and small brochure sites
- require source citations in every memory-based answer

## Why

- local notes are cheap, inspectable and versionable
- deterministic behavior is easier to audit than an opaque cloud stack
- narrow scope reduces delivery risk and founder distraction

## Coach Signals

- do not expand into multi-agent automation before the offer is clearer
- strengthen the sales and offer learning loop before scaling tooling
