# Business Brief

## Project

Web Studio OS is the internal operating system of a micro-agency that sells fast websites and small automations to small businesses.

## Problem

The founder loses time on repeated work: lead qualification, scoping, SEO framing, email drafts, admin follow-up and project memory.

## Offer Direction

- sell clear MVP website offers first
- avoid custom builds with vague scope
- turn repeated founder knowledge into reusable notes

## Coach Signals

- learning should support revenue in the next 30 days
- avoid deep technical rabbit holes with no direct sales impact
- prefer skills that reduce sales friction and delivery risk
