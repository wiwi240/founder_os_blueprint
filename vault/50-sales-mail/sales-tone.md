# Sales Tone

## Tone Principles

- clear
- calm
- direct
- low-hype
- useful

## Rules

- do not overpromise on price or timeline
- explain tradeoffs instead of hiding them
- ask short scoping questions
- speak in client outcomes, not developer jargon

## Coach Signals

- learn concise sales writing
- learn discovery questions that reduce ambiguity
- learn to answer objections without sounding defensive
